How it works.!?

Step 1.

Hit the "START" button in the home to re-direct to the image upload page.

Step 2.

Hit the "UPLOAD" button to upload images from your gallery or you can take a photo too and select the number of dominating colors.

Step 3.

Hit the "EXTRACT" button to get the color codes, This will load a while. After that you will get color code in Hex and RBG format.
